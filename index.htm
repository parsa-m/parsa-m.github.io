Parsa Mahmoudieh

I am currently working on intersections of Reinforcement Learning, NLP, and Computer Vision research at Google Deepmind in Mountain View. My team's latest work on LearnLM was presented at Google I/O 2024 here!

I finished my CS PhD at UC Berkeley in BAIR advised by Trevor Darrell and have been mentored by Deepak Pathak and Evan Shelhamer. I have also worked with Pulkit Agrawal, Alyosha Efros, and Jitendra Malik.

Before grad school, I did a double major in EECS and MechE at UC Berkeley and had done undergraduate research in Ron Fearing's Robotics lab. I've also done internships at GM and Ford.
